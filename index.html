The Knowledge of Water

Jal Veda, from the Sanskrit for "knowledge of water," was founded on a simple principle: technology must serve our most fundamental human needs. We are a team of scientists, engineers, and community organizers dedicated to solving India's water crisis by democratizing access to hydrogeological data.

Our approach is not just about conservation; it's about empowerment. By placing scientifically-backed tools directly into the hands of farmers and local communities, we are building a decentralized, grassroots movement for a sustainable future.

A Silent Crisis Beneath Our Feet

Groundwater depletion isn't just an environmental issue; it's a critical threat to our economy, food security, and the well-being of millions.

Agricultural Collapse

Farmers face crop failure as millions of borewells run dry, jeopardizing food supply chains.

Economic Instability

Industries from manufacturing to energy rely on water. Scarcity puts economic growth at risk.

Community Displacement

Rural communities are forced to migrate as their primary source of life and livelihood disappears.

Ecological Damage

Depleting aquifers lead to land subsidence, desertification, and irreversible damage to ecosystems.

Our Technology-Driven Ecosystem

Jal Veda has built a suite of tools and communities to tackle the water crisis from every angle.

Jal Sevak AI

For Farmers: Your AI Hydrologist, Free on WhatsApp.

We put the power of a hydrogeologist in every farmer's pocket. Our AI chatbot delivers a free, personalized, and scientifically-backed borewell recharge plan by analyzing satellite, geological, and rainfall data in real-time.

Jal Mitra

For the Community: A Network of Water Stewards.

The Jal Mitra program connects farmers with a network of verified local suppliers, contractors, and fellow "Water Friends." This community ensures quality construction and fosters a grassroots movement of sustainable practices.

Jal Veda Platform

For Partners: A PaaS for Scalable Impact.

Our enterprise-grade Platform-as-a-Service allows NGOs and government agencies to deploy our technology at scale. Plan, monitor, and report on large-scale water projects with unparalleled precision and transparency.
